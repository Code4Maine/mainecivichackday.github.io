About Us

History

2013 inagurated successful civic hackathons in both Bangor and Portland.

Philosophy

Working toward a shared goal can transform a community. In our midst we have the talent, we have the desire, what we need is to come together to face our common challenges.

Motivation

To create an opportunity for unexpected and surprisingly solutions to emerge. To demonstrate positive patterns in community problem solving. To inspire and energize.

Problems for 2014

Actual Problems from Real People.

The core principle of our event is that we only work on actual problems that are validated by people working in the field, or by people who experience the pain caused by the problem. We then work with these individuals or organization to define their problem in the form of a challenge to which the community can respond with a prototype solution. Each problem will be presented by a live human being who will be eager and interested to see a prototype solution mature into deployment and maintenance.
