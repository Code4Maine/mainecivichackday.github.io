About Us

History

2013 inagurated what will hopefully be many years of successful civic hackathons in both Bangor and Portland.

Philosophy

Working toward a shared goal can transform a community. In our midst we have the talent, we have the desire, what we need is to come together to face our common challenges.

Motivation

To create an opportunity for unexpected and surprisingly solutions to emerge. To demonstrate positive patterns in community problem solving. To inspire and energize.

Launched in 2013

Catalyzing Maine's technical community.

Pushing code.

Pulling in partners

Join us and shape the experience.

Participate

How can you help?

Civic hacking requires people. No matter what your technical skills, no matter how much hacking you've done in the past, we want you!

Join us on May 31!

The most obvious way to participate is to register for the event over the weekend of May 31 - June 1.

Help organize

There are many important things that must get done for the event to be a success. Please join us on Github where we manage tasks.

Sponsor the event

Please consider sponsoring us via either food, funds or space to make this year's event a huge success.

Sponsorship

Help us help Maine.

We are all volunteers. Even volunteers need coffee and food when the spend a weekend hacking on projects that will improve civic life across the pine tree state. Consider sponsoring our event.
