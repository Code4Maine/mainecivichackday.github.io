Maine Civic Hack Day

Hacking for a Better Tomorrow, May 31st 2014

Schedule

We have events in Bangor, and Portland this year.  Find the schedule for your event below:

Bangor

Saturday May 31st

  • 7:30 AM – Registration Opens, Breakfast is served
  • 8:30 AM – Welcome & Opening Address
  • 8:45 AM – Problem Definitions Presented
  • 9:15 AM – Partipant pitches & team formation
  • 9:45 AM – Workshops Round 1 (Databases & Source Control)
  • 10:15 AM – Workshops Round 2 (Civic Hacking in Maine, Working with OpenStreetMap)
  • 11:00 AM – Hacking Officially Begins!
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch is served
  • 2:00 PM – Coffee & Snacks
  • 5:00 PM – Dinner is served
  • 8:00 PM – Hacking continues into the night!

Sunday June 1st

  • 8:30 AM – Doors Open & Breakfast is served
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch is served
  • 2:00 PM – Coffee & Snacks
  • 3:30 PM – Demos Start
  • 4:30 PM – Demos conclude, Prizes!
  • 5:00 PM – So long, farewell, Hurrah!

Note: For members of the community or local government who are interested in “pitching” problems but may not be able to stay for the entire weekend, the most valuable times to come are 10:00a-12:00p on Saturday, and 6:00p-8:00p on Sunday. Any additional time you can give to “check in” with the group and answer questions, guide progress, and provide feedback is greatly appreciated.
